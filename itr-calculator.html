Which regime is better for you?

Choose New Regime if...

  • ✓ Income up to ₹12 Lakh (zero tax)
  • ✓ You have few or no deductions
  • ✓ You don't invest in 80C instruments
  • ✓ You want simplicity & lower compliance
  • ✓ First-time taxpayers or young earners

Choose Old Regime if...

  • ✓ You have large deductions (80C, HRA, home loan)
  • ✓ Deductions exceed ₹3.75 Lakh+
  • ✓ High income with lots of investments
  • ✓ Senior citizens with higher basic exemption
  • ✓ Businesses with allowable expenses
