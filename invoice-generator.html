GST Invoice Requirements

A valid GST tax invoice must include: GSTIN, invoice number, date, HSN/SAC code, GST rate, CGST/SGST or IGST breakup.

💾

How to Save as PDF

Click "Print / Save PDF" → In the print dialog, select "Save as PDF" as the printer → Click Save. Works on all browsers.

⚖️

Invoice Numbering

Use a sequential format like KAS/2026-27/001. The series must restart each financial year. Must be unique per GSTIN.
