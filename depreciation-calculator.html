WDV vs SLM — Which to use?

• WDV (Written Down Value) — Used for Income Tax Act. Higher depreciation in early years. More tax benefit initially.
• SLM (Straight Line Method) — Used for Companies Act. Equal depreciation each year. Simpler for accounting.
• Most businesses use WDV for IT returns and SLM for financial statements.
