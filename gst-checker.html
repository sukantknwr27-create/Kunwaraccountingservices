Consider COMPOSITION SCHEME
You may qualify for the Composition Scheme
GST registration is mandatory, but you may be eligible for the Composition Scheme — a simpler option with lower tax rates.

Composition Scheme benefits (2026)

  • Pay tax at flat rate — 1% traders, 5% restaurants, 6% services
  • File only 1 return per quarter (CMP-08) + annual GSTR-4
  • Much simpler bookkeeping and compliance
  • Significantly lower tax burden for small businesses

Composition Scheme limitations

  • Only for turnover up to ₹1.5 Crore
  • Cannot do inter-state sales of goods
  • Cannot sell on Amazon, Flipkart etc.
  • Cannot claim Input Tax Credit on purchases

Tax rates under Composition Scheme

Manufacturers & Traders1% of turnover
Restaurants5% of turnover
Service Providers6% of turnover
Returns to fileCMP-08 quarterly + GSTR-4 annually
