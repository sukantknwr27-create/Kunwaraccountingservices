What is CGST + SGST?

For sales within the same state, GST is split equally between Central GST (CGST) and State GST (SGST). Both appear on your invoice.

What is IGST?

For inter-state sales (selling to another state), Integrated GST (IGST) applies. It equals the full GST rate and goes to the central government.

GST on services (2026)

Most professional services (accounting, IT, consulting, legal) attract 18% GST in 2026. Always collect GST from business clients.
